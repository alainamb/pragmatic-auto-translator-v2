Further Details

Understanding the Visualizations

  • Clustering: Points close together represent semantically similar content
  • Cross-lingual alignment: Similar content from each language appears near each other
  • Color patterns: Each language has its own color scale, with darker shades for higher-level content (documents) and lighter shades for details (paragraphs)
  • Interactive exploration: Hover over points to see detailed information, including the document or section of the vector
  • All popups: Show corpus item IDs and titles
  • Section/Paragraph popups: Include excerpts to further facilitate lookup of content

Technical Parameters

Model Architecture: Latest transformer architecture with enhanced multilingual training

Training Data: Optimized for cross-lingual similarity and retrieval tasks

Vector Quality: L2 normalized for consistent similarity calculations

Performance: Enhanced accuracy for multilingual semantic similarity

Dimensionality Reduction: PCA (Principal Component Analysis) preserves maximum variance while enabling visualization
